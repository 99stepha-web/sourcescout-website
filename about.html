SourceScout is an independent product-discovery publication focused on helping readers navigate the enormous selection available across global online marketplaces.

Why SourceScout exists

Online marketplaces make it possible to discover products from suppliers and brands around the world. But having more choices does not always make buying easier.

Product listings can contain incomplete information, promotional pricing, unfamiliar sellers and specifications that are difficult to compare.

SourceScout was created to make product discovery more useful. We identify interesting products and categories, organize available information and highlight the questions buyers should consider before making a purchase.

How we research products

Our research may consider publicly available product information including specifications, pricing, customer feedback, seller information and comparable alternatives.

Technology, including automated data analysis and artificial intelligence tools, may assist our research and organization process. However, automated analysis does not replace independent verification.

Unless we explicitly state that a product has been personally tested, readers should not interpret our coverage as a hands-on product review.

Our editorial principles

We aim to distinguish between information provided by sellers, our own analysis and information that still requires verification.

Prices, availability, shipping terms and product details can change at any time. Readers should always verify important information directly with the marketplace or seller before purchasing.
